YUNI Summer Camp 2026
Future Skills, Creativity & Career Exploration Program
Program Structure
Duration: 6 WeeksClasses: 3–4 Sessions per WeekClass Duration: 1.5 Hours per SessionLevel: Beginner to Intermediate
YUNI Summer Camp is designed to introduce students to high-demand skills through hands-on learning, guided projects, competitions, and portfolio development. Each track serves as a foundation pathway into advanced professional training programs.
